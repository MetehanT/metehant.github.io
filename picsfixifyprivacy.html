Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 05.02.2024 (February 5, 2025)

At PicsFixify, we highly value user privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data. PicsFixify does not store personal data or retain uploaded photos after processing.

This policy is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other global data protection laws.

1. Data Collection and Usage

1.1. Personal Information

PicsFixify does not collect personally identifiable information. However, we use third-party services for certain functionalities:

  • Firebase Authentication: Used for user login. If you sign in via Google, Apple, or other providers, Firebase may provide identity details (e.g., email).
  • Purchases: In-app purchases are handled through Apple App Store payment systems. PicsFixify does not store credit card or payment details.

1.2. Photo Data

  • PicsFixify does not store uploaded photos.
  • Photos are processed using Replicate.com AI models and automatically deleted after processing.

1.3. Analytics and Crash Tracking

  • We use Firebase Crashlytics for performance tracking and bug resolution.
  • Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve the user experience.

1.4. Advertisements

  • PicsFixify may display ads through Google AdMob.
  • Users can disable personalized ads via their device settings.

2. Third-Party Services

PicsFixify integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (User login)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash tracking)
  • Firebase AdMob (Ad display)
  • Firebase Database & Functions (App functionalities)
  • Replicate.com (AI-powered photo enhancement)

These services have their own privacy policies. Please review them for more information.
