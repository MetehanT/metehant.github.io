Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2025

At MakeToon: Anime Maker, we care deeply about your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

MakeToon: Anime Maker does not store personal data or uploaded photos after processing. Our core functionality is transforming your photos into anime or cartoon-style characters using artificial intelligence.

This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other major data protection laws.

1. What Data We Collect and Why

1.1. Account and Login Data

  • We use Firebase Authentication for optional sign-in via Google, Apple, or other providers.
  • We may receive non-sensitive info such as your email or name from Firebase if you choose to sign in.
  • Login is not required to use our app’s core features.

1.2. Photo & Face Data

  • We do not store or save your photos or face data.
  • Photos are used only to apply anime/cartoon filters and effects.
  • Facial features are detected to apply stylistic changes like anime eyes, cartoon outlines, and skin tone stylization.
  • All processing is done temporarily and automatically deleted afterward.
  • Processing is powered by Fal.ai, which is contractually required to delete data immediately after use.

1.3. Payments

  • In-app purchases are handled via the Apple App Store.
  • We do not collect or store your payment or credit card details.

1.4. Analytics & Crash Reports

  • We use Firebase Crashlytics to track bugs and app performance.
  • Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve the app experience.

1.5. Advertisements

  • Ads may be shown through Google AdMob.
  • You can disable personalized ads via your device’s ad settings.

1.6. Face Data Collection and Processing

MakeToon: Anime Maker processes face data from uploaded photos to create anime or cartoon-style portraits. Here's how we handle it:

Purpose of Use

  • Face data is used only to detect facial features for the purpose of applying artistic effects and transformations (e.g., anime-style eyes, cartoon outlines).
  • We do not use face data for identification, authentication, or surveillance.

Storage and Retention

  • No Permanent Storage: Face data is never stored on our servers.
  • Temporary Processing: Face data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after the transformation is complete.

Third-Party Processing

  • Face data is processed using Fal.ai AI models for transformation only.
  • Fal.ai is contractually required to delete all data immediately after processing.
  • We do not share face data with advertisers or any other third parties.

Compliance

  • Our handling of face data complies with Apple’s privacy requirements, GDPR, and CCPA.
  • Face data is not used for user profiling or tracking.

  • Fal.ai (photo processing & AI transformations)
  • Google AdMob (advertisements)

These partners have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

3. Data Security

  • We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Photo and face data are not stored after processing.
  • Firebase services use Google’s secure infrastructure.

4. Your Rights

  • You can delete your account at any time.
  • You can turn off personalized ads from your device settings.
  • If you are located in the EU or California, you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data in accordance with GDPR/CCPA.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. You will be notified in the app or via our website if changes occur.
