Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17.02.2024 (February 17, 2025)

At Ghiblify, we highly value user privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data. Ghiblify does not store personal data or retain uploaded photos after processing.

This policy is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other global data protection laws.

1. Data Collection and Usage

1.1. Personal Information

Ghiblify does not collect personally identifiable information. However, we use third-party services for certain functionalities:

  • Firebase Authentication: Used for user login. If you sign in via Google, Apple, or other providers, Firebase may provide identity details (e.g., email).
  • Purchases: In-app purchases are handled through Apple App Store payment systems. Ghiblify does not store credit card or payment details.

1.2. Photo Data

  • Ghiblify does not store uploaded photos.
  • Photos are processed using Fal.ai AI models and automatically deleted after processing.

1.3. Face Data Collection and Processing

Ghiblify may process photos containing facial features for enhancement purposes. Here's how we handle face data:

Collection and Use

  • Face data is only processed for the specific purpose of photo enhancement and beautification.
  • We use AI models to detect and enhance facial features such as skin smoothing, portrait enhancement, and facial feature optimization.
  • Face data processing is performed solely to deliver the core functionality of our photo enhancement service.

Storage and Retention

  • No Permanent Storage: Ghiblify does not permanently store any face data or facial features.
  • Temporary Processing: Face data is only temporarily processed during the enhancement operation and is immediately deleted after processing is complete.
  • Maximum Retention: Face data is retained only for the duration of the processing operation, which typically takes a few seconds to minutes.

Third-Party Sharing

  • Face data is processed using Fal.ai's AI models for enhancement purposes only.
  • We do not share face data with any other third parties.
  • Our AI processing partner, Fal.ai, is contractually bound to delete all processed data immediately after the enhancement operation.

1.4. Analytics and Crash Tracking

  • We use Firebase Crashlytics for performance tracking and bug resolution.
  • Anonymous usage data may be collected to improve the user experience.

1.5. Advertisements

  • Ghiblify may display ads through Google AdMob.
  • Users can disable personalized ads via their device settings.

2. Third-Party Services

Ghiblify integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash tracking)
  • Firebase AdMob (Ad display)
  • Firebase Database & Functions (App functionalities)
  • Fal.ai (AI-powered photo enhancement)

These services have their own privacy policies. Please review them for more information.

3. Data Security

  • Ghiblify applies industry-standard security measures to protect user data.
  • Photos are not retained after processing.
  • Firebase services are secured with Google's advanced security infrastructure.

4. Compliance with Regional Data Protection Laws

4.1. GDPR (EU/EEA)

  • Ghiblify complies with GDPR.
  • Users can opt-in or opt-out of personal data processing.

4.2. CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

  • California residents have the right to access, modify, and delete their data.

4.3. Other Countries

Ghiblify adheres to relevant data protection laws in various regions.

5. User Rights and Choices

  • Users can delete their accounts at any time.
  • Users can disable personalized ads through device settings.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
